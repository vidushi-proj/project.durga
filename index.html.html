Durga Within Every Girl

Who Are We?

Project Durga is a bold, youth-led awareness and advocacy platform reimagining gender justice in Indian society. We are a collective of young changemakers using storytelling, education, and public dialogue to challenge patriarchal structures and empower marginalized voices.

Rooted in SDG 5 — Gender Equality, our mission goes beyond gender. We advocate for intersectional justice through:

  • SDG 3 — Mental health & well-being for marginalized genders
  • SDG 4 — Access to inclusive, feminist education
  • SDG 10 — Reducing social, caste & gender-based inequality

Because justice is not siloed. And awareness is our loudest tool.

Our Mission

To build an India where every girl recognizes the Durga within her — fierce, resilient, and unstoppable. We aim to empower youth through education, creativity, and community action, challenging gender norms and fostering inclusive change.

Meet the Founder

Vidushi Sharma is a young changemaker who believes the fiercest revolutions begin with a story. From slam poetry to grassroots advocacy, her journey is rooted in the belief that courage is contagious. Project Durga is her call to the youth — to speak, to act, and to redefine equality for our times.
